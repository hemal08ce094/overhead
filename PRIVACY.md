# Privacy Policy — Overhead

**Effective date:** July 4, 2026

Overhead ("the app," "we," "us") is built to collect as little as possible. There is **no account, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking**. This policy explains exactly what data the app uses and where it goes.

## The short version

- No account and no sign-in.
- We run **no servers of our own** and keep **none** of your data.
- Everything you create in the app — favorites, spotting stats, medals — stays **on your device**.
- We never sell, rent, or share your personal data, and we don't track you across other apps or websites.

## Location

Overhead uses your device's precise location to place aircraft and celestial objects at their true positions relative to you, and to compute local sky events (such as eclipse timing and visibility).

To show the aircraft near you, the app sends your **approximate coordinates** to public flight‑data providers (see *Third‑party services* below) so they can return the traffic in your area. These requests contain your location and, for a given flight, its callsign or identifier — but **no name, account, device identifier, or anything that identifies you**. We do not operate these providers and do not receive, log, or store these requests; they go directly from your device to the provider.

If you decline location access, the app still works in a demo / dark‑sky mode.

## Camera

In augmented‑reality mode, Overhead uses the camera to show the live sky behind the on‑screen labels. The camera image is processed **on your device in real time and is never recorded, saved, or transmitted**. You can use the app fully with the camera off (Dark sky mode).

## Data stored on your device

Favorites, spotting statistics, medals and tiers, and your settings are stored **only on your device** and are never uploaded. Deleting the app removes them.

## Third‑party services

To provide flight and sky data, the app makes requests to the following independent services. Each has its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review:

| Service | Purpose | Data sent |
|---|---|---|
| **airplanes.live** | Live aircraft positions | Your approximate location (to return nearby traffic) |
| **adsbdb** | Flight routes | Flight callsign |
| **planespotters.net** | Aircraft photos | Aircraft type / registration |
| **CelesTrak** | ISS orbital data | None |

We do not control these services and are not responsible for their practices.

## What we don't do

- No analytics or usage‑tracking SDKs.
- No advertising and no advertising identifiers.
- No data brokers — we never sell or share your data.
- No cross‑app or cross‑site tracking. (We don't use App Tracking Transparency because we don't track you.)

## Children's privacy

Overhead does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+.
